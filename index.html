TWICE 5TH WORLD TOUR 'READY TO BE' IN SINGAPORE will take place at the Singapore Indoor Stadium on 2 September 2023 (Saturday). Live Nation members can secure tickets first during the exclusive Live Nation presale on 7 June (Wed) from 10am till 11:59pm. Visit www.livenation.sg for free membership signup and presale access. Tickets to public will be available from 8 June (Thu), 10am onwards via www.ticketmaster.sg. VIP tickets will be available for purchase for enthusiastic fans to gain access to the soundcheck party, and each VIP ticket holder will also receive a commemorative laminate with lanyard.
